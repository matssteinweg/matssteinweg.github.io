Biography

I'm a master thesis student at the Robotics and Perception Group under the supervision of Prof. Davide Scaramuzza. My research interests are mainly centered around perception and control for autonomous mobile robots. In my current research I'm investigating the potential of deep reinforcement learning for time-optimal control in autonomous drone racing.
